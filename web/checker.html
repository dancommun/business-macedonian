Honest limit: this is a pattern checker, not a grammar engine. It catches the tells it has been taught (known calques, over-used connectors, typography) — it cannot see a missing clitic, a wrong verb tense, or a calque it hasn't learned. A clean result means "no known tells", not "certified native". The real gate is reading it aloud: would a Macedonian actually say this?
